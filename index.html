Kino Beats×J-Willz
Jamaica × Norfolk  ·  Charlottesville

Kino Beats — a Jamaica-born, Norfolk-raised producer who cut his teeth touring 20+ countries as Lex Luger's DJ before landing credits alongside Pharrell on A$AP Rocky's "Hear Me" and producing for Jeezy, Larry June, and Curren$y.

J-Willz — Charlottesville's Golden Child, a rapper who was opening for MGK, Tyga, and Waka Flocka before most artists his age had a SoundCloud page.

Together, they created "Something from Nothing" — the flagship anthem for OneState, the social roleplay phenomenon where millions of players build their own stories, earn their status, and live a life with no script.

This isn't a corporate soundtrack. This is the sound of a world where everyone starts from nothing — and makes it everything.
